Here's a time line of Ayrton Senna's life:

  • 1960 - born in São Paulo
  • 1975 - Started his carrer as a Brazilian racing driver in Karting
  • 1983 - began racing professionally and won the Formula 3 British championship
  • 1984 - He started testing in the F1, all the teams saw potential in him.
  • 1985 - Senna started to race in Formula 1 with Lotus F1 team. Everyone was surprised with Senna, he had a true talent.
  • 1987 - He became a true hero for Brazil, everyone looked up to him, he was Brazils pride.
  • 1990 - Senna was a very good driver, but even a better person. Thats why he was a very loved person, he made donations of over 400 million dolars of his own money to help children poverty in Brazil.
  • 1993 - He raced for Brazil, and he tried to help Brazil as much as he could. He was an inspiration to many kids and teenagers in Brazil.
  • 1994 - This year, he crashed and died in a race. It was a terrible day for many people around the world. Even after he died he left a legacy behind, because of his crash, many new safty regulations were introduced to F1, wich has helped save the lives of many drivers.
